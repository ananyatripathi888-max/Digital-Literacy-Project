Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts
Do's
1.	Keep your profile professional and up to date at all times.
2.	Use privacy settings to control who can see your content and personal information.
3.	Use social media positively — to network, learn, and follow industry professionals.
4.	Report any harassment, hate speech, or suspicious accounts you come across.
5.	Separate your personal and professional accounts wherever possible.



Don'ts
1.	Don't post anything offensive, discriminatory, or hurtful toward any individual or group.
2.	Don't share personal details such as your address, phone number, or daily routine publicly.
3.	Don't spread unverified news, rumours, or misleading information.
4.	Don't post about others — including photos or videos — without their knowledge or consent.
5.	Don't accept follow or friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
